# How to make my favorite Tea in pseudocode

	BEGAN
	
		GET kettle
	
		WHILE kettle not full
	
			ADD water to kettle
	
		PLACE kettle on heat
	
		WHILE water not boiling
	
			WAIT
	
		GET cup
	
		GET tea bag
	
		PLACE tea bag in cup
	
		PLACE water in cup
	
		WAIT 10 minutes
	
		REMOVE tea bag from cup
	
		GET sugar
	
		ADD Sugar to cup
	
		ADD Sugar to cup
	
		ADD Sugar to cup
	
		GET honey
	
		ADD honey to cup
	
		ADD Sugar to cup
	
		ADD Sugar to cup

  		// Adding Sugar for comedic effect
	
	END

 enjoy your Tea!
